Hi there! I’m Michael Richmond V. Racelis who loves turning creative ideas into something real and meaningful. Art has always been my way of expressing myself and sharing what I’m passionate about with others. Whether it’s through bold designs, intricate sketches, or experimenting with something new, I’m always striving to create work that inspires and connects.

About my Hobby

Photography is something I’ve always been drawn to. From capturing the beauty of nature to the simple moments with friends, it’s a way for me to express myself and hold onto memories. What I love most about photography is the way it makes me feel connected to the world around me. Every photo I take is a way to tell a story or capture something beautiful. It allows me to see things from different perspectives and to appreciate the small details in life. photography has become an integral part of my life. It’s not just about taking pictures; it’s about capturing emotions, stories, and memories that would otherwise be lost. Every shot I take is a reminder of how beautiful and fleeting life can be. The more I explore photography, the more I realize how much there is to learn and how much it helps me connect with the world around me. It’s a passion that will continue to grow and inspire me for years to come.

Drawing is something I've always been passionate about. It's an activity that allows me to express my creativity and bring my ideas to life. Whether it’s through a simple sketch or a detailed illustration, drawing helps me relax and think in new ways. I love drawing because it lets me escape into a world of imagination. The process of starting with a blank page and slowly bringing it to life with lines and colors is incredibly satisfying. Every drawing is like a story, and each one has a piece of my personality and thoughts in it. It's not just about the final product but the journey of creating something from nothing. Drawing is more than just a hobby for me; it’s a form of self-expression and a way to communicate my ideas without words. It brings me joy, challenges me, and lets me see the world from different perspectives. I hope to continue exploring and improving my skills, and who knows where this love for drawing might take me in the future.

Handcrafting is a passion that allows me to connect with materials and techniques in a very personal way. It’s about creating something meaningful with my own hands, whether it’s a piece of art, a functional object, or a gift for someone special. Every project is an opportunity to learn and improve my skills, and I find joy in the process of bringing ideas to life. Handcrafting, for me, is about the joy of creating something personal and unique. I’ve always had a love for fashion, and making clothes allows me to express myself in a way that feels both creative and practical. Whether it’s designing a new shirt, altering a piece to make it my own, or learning new sewing techniques, I love how handcrafting clothes allows me to merge functionality with style. Handcrafts bring me a sense of joy and satisfaction that few other activities can match. Whether I’m creating something functional or purely decorative, I love the challenge and the creativity involved. Handcrafting is my way of slowing down, focusing, and connecting with my artistic side.

Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby

- Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby: - A cellphone with a decent camera. - A good place with natural light. - Inspiration or a subject to capture.

- Steps to Get Started: 1. Find a well-lit location or an interesting subject. 2. Explore your cellphone's camera settings and practice with angles. 3. Experiment with editing apps to enhance your shots.

- Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby: - A comfortable place to draw. - A sketchbook for ideas and practice. - Art materials like pencils, erasers, and markers.

- Steps to Get Started: 1. Gather your sketchbook and art tools. 2. Choose a quiet place to focus and relax. 3. Start sketching simple objects and build up to more complex designs.

- Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby: - Clothes to redesign or upcycle. - Sewing tools like needles, thread, and fabric scissors. - Customizing tools like paint, patches, or embroidery kits.

- Steps to Get Started: 1. Select clothes you want to repurpose or redesign. 2. Plan your design and gather all necessary tools. 3. Start sewing or customizing, taking your time to perfect your craft.
